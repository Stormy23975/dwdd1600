Dizzy’s Key Moves

Dizzy controls the pacing with freeze effects, summons, and long-range slashes, rewarding patient zoning and smart setups.

This section will cover the following:

  1. Fast and Safe Normals
  2. Core Special Moves
  3. Overdrive Attacks

Fast and Safe Normals

Crouching Punch (2P)
Dizzy’s fastest button—great for interrupting pressure.
Far Slash (fS)
A poke from afar that keeps opponents at bay and can combo into Michael Sword or freezing setups on counter hit.
Crouching Dust (2D)
Long-reaching, ideal for catching advancing foes and creating knockdown setups with Ice Fish.

Core Special Moves

Ice Fish (“We Talked A Lot Together”)
A summon that starts Dizzy’s offense. The ground version crawls forward, useful for covering approaches and forcing the opponent to block, while the air version flies higher controlling aerial space and stopping jumps. Both are key to her pressure.
Ice Pillar (“I Used This To Catch Fish”)
A ranged ice projectile that freezes on hit, setting up combos or transitions into Ice Field.
Ice Field
Covers the ground in ice, making movement slippery and extending Dizzy’s control of the screen.
Michael Sword
A long-range slash—fast version for combos, heavy version for punishing careless movement.
Wings of Light
A delayed, unblockable zone attack that drains Tension to deal continuous damage once fully active.

Overdrive Attacks

Imperial Ray
An invincible ice blast that freezes opponents, great for reversals and corner setups.
Gamma Ray
Dizzy’s 100% Tension Overdrive—fires a devastating beam with Necro and Undine, dealing massive damage.
