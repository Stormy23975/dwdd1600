Dizzy Overview

Introducing the queen herself!

Dizzy is a beloved character from the Guilty Gear series, often recognized for her angelic wings and gentle nature that contrast with the chaos around her.
In Guilty Gear Strive, she plays as a versatile zoner, using long-range projectiles and summoned creatures to control space and keep opponents at a distance. While she can apply pressure with her unique tools, she generally rewards patient, strategic play rather than rushing in headfirst.
